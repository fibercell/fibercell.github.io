Natural Fiber Technology

Pinawaste is a social entity in Malaya Village consisting of women, widows, and marginalized groups. We started forming groups since early 2019, starting by analyzing problems in society and finding solutions so that we succeeded in bringing Sungai Malaya Village, which was originally a disadvantaged village, to become an independent village today with the innovations that we developed together. Our group consists of three teams, namely extraction, harvesting, and derivative product processing units.
