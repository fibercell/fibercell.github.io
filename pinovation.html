Eco-Pads

Eco Green Menstrual Pad (Ecopads) is an environmentally friendly menstrual pad that uses pineapple leaf fiber as its main ingredient. Ecopads are made using cellulose derived from pineapple leaf fiber that is processed into a bioadsorbent, making it a disposable pad that is different from other similar products. The uniqueness of Ecopads lies in its ability to decompose naturally in the soil. The waterproof layer on Ecopads uses a material synthesized from discarded cassava (grade 2 unfit for consumption) which is then processed into bioplastic. The result is a waterproof film layer and based on degradation tests, this layer only takes about 53 weeks to decompose naturally.

Sustainable Arts & Culture

Biodegradable Vegan Leather from pineapple fiber has almost the same characteristics as animal skin, hard and can be carved depending on the manufacturing technique.

Vegan leather is an alternative material to animal leather, made from non-animal sources, designed to support sustainability and reduce environmental impact. We chose pineapple fiber as the main material for vegan leather because the surplus of pineapple leaves, often left unused or burned, contributes to environmental pollution. In our region, the surplus of pineapple fiber reaches up to 150 kg per month, presenting a significant opportunity to transform this agricultural waste into high-value products. This initiative aligns with the sustainable fashion campaign, which aims to reduce dependence on animal leather, minimize the carbon footprint of the fashion industry, and promote eco-friendly materials for a greener future.

Wayang Kulit that we made from vegan leather is a modern innovation that combines cultural heritage with eco-friendly materials. Crafted from pineapple fibers processed into vegan leather, these wayang puppets preserve the beauty of traditional art while promoting sustainability. The use of vegan leather replaces animal hides, making it an ethical and eco-friendly choice, celebrating cultural heritage in a form that aligns with global efforts to protect the environment.
